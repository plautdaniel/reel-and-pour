Cinema
Reel & Pour is a community-curated monthly film club that puts the audience in charge. Each month, three films are selected through a three-channel curation system — drawing on what members want to watch, what's playing now, and which directors deserve a deeper look. Members watch on their own schedule, then gather every month on a Thursday evening to share reactions, debate interpretations, and enjoy a pint together.
Curation System
Three Signals. One Monthly List.

Each month's three films are drawn from a combination of these inputs — not one from each, but whatever the signals point toward. Some months that's a theme that emerges across all three. Others, it's pure watchlist consensus or a director worth spending three films on together.

01
The Watchlist
Members submit as many films as they like. Each month, submissions are analyzed to surface shared interest. Films with the highest overlap across members are strong candidates for selection.
02
Now Playing
Current theatrical releases are factored in — both as a reason to go to the cinema together and as a thematic anchor. A new film can pull adjacent watchlist titles into focus around a shared subject.
03
Director Deep Dive
Members nominate directors worth exploring. A filmmaker's work can shape one selection, or color the whole month if the watchlist and theaters happen to align around the same voice or era.

Monthly Selection
The Output
Three Films. Your Timeline.
Each month's three selections are shared with members ahead of the gathering. Everyone watches on their own — at home, at the cinema, however works for them. No required screening night. Just a common list and a shared deadline: Thursday evening.
The Event
Thursday Night at the Beer Garden
🍺
Venue City State Brewery — a great spot for an evening of film talk and a well-earned pint.
🕖
Time Every month, Thursday evening from 7:45 – 10:00 PM. Arrive with opinions. Leave with recommendations.
🎬
Format Open discussion across all three films — no formal agenda. Themes, performances, direction, what worked, what didn't. The conversation leads.
🗓
Cadence Monthly. A watchlist goes out at the start of the month. The gathering caps it off. Then it starts again.
Participation
How It Works
  1. 1 Fill out the Watchlist Form — add every film you've been meaning to see, and nominate some directors you'd like to explore. The more the better; overlap is the point.
  2. 2 At the start of each month, receive the three-film selection.
  3. 3 Watch what you can, in whatever order, on your own schedule before the gathering.
  4. 4 Show up Thursday. Order a drink. Talk about films with people who watched the same ones.
